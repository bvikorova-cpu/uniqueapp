# QA — Virtual Influencer Agency (P0)

**URL:** `/virtual-influencer-agency` | **Kredity:** 5+ / akcia

## A. Prístup
1. `/virtual-influencer-agency` → hero, "Create influencer" CTA, marketplace, brand deals
2. Gate
3. Mobile

## B. Tvorba persony
1. Klik Create → wizard
2. Step 1: meno, vek, gender, ethnicity
3. Step 2: avatar — AI generate alebo upload base
4. Step 3: personality traits (energetic/calm/sassy)
5. Step 4: niche (fitness/fashion/tech/lifestyle/travel)
6. Step 5: posting frequency (denne/3x týždeň)
7. Step 6: tone of voice
8. Submit → kredity, persona v "My Influencers"

## C. Auto-posting
1. AI generuje posty podľa schedule
2. Preview post pred publish → approve/reject/edit
3. Manual override post
4. Skip post → next slot
5. Image generation per post → kredity per image

## D. Follower simulácia
1. Spustenie persony → simulovaný organic growth
2. Likes/comments AI generated
3. Real users môžu followovať (cross-user)
4. Engagement dashboard

## E. Brand deals
1. "Brand deals" tab → list ponúk od značiek
2. Klik deal → detail (kompenzácia, requirements)
3. Accept → persona vytvorí sponsored post, revenue na účet
4. Decline
5. Brand strana (iný user) → ponúkne deal → A dostane notifikáciu

## F. Marketplace
1. Browse personas of others
2. Hire (rent) persona for campaign → owner revenue
3. Rating systém

## G. Analytics
1. Per-persona dashboard: followers, engagement rate, revenue
2. Top posts
3. Demographics
4. Export CSV report

## H. Kredity & monetization
1. Create: 5
2. Post: 3-5 per image
3. Brand deal revenue 85/15 split
4. Hire fee revenue split

## I. Multi-user
1. A create persona → public visible
2. B follow → A persona followers +1
3. B comment → A persona auto-reply (AI)
4. Brand C ponúka deal A → notifikácia real-time
5. Concurrent 10 followers commenting → AI handles bez crash

## J. Moderation
1. NSFW post prevention
2. Hate speech filter
3. Brand safety pre deals (no controversial niches)
4. Report persona → admin review

## K. Security
1. RLS na personas
2. Brand deal contracts immutable po accept
3. Payment escrow do completion

## L. i18n
1. Posts v jazyku persony (configurable)
2. UI translation
